Some shader properties explanation:
Bend By View - bend a top part of grass mesh in direction of a camera. Allow to achive more dense look of a grass. 0.0 - no bending, 0.5-1.0 - reasonable bending value
Grass Bottom(Y) - bottom plane of grass mesh. Height that under of it grass don't be waving at all. 0.0 is most common value.
Grass Top(Y) - top plane of a grass. Height that above of it grass will be waving at maximum power. 1.0 is most common value.

Other properties is the same as the standart unity grass.
